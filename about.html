ABOUT

OUR CAUSE:

To create a judge-free place to share art and music.

Our goal is to create a website which appreciates and includes all forms of music and art, hence our name M+A. Our website can be used as a place for aspiring artists to share their work with the public or just someone who wants to put there work on the internet. We do not have a comment section, which helps artists to upload and share their work without inhibitions. The weekly hits are determined by the artworks that receive the largest number of stars, but an artist can only see the number of stars their artwork has got, not how many the works of other artists have got. This is in hopes to create an environment without the comparing of numbers, but instead the admiration of art. Hope you enjoy exploring our website!
